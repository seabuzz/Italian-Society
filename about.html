Our Mission

We organise film screenings, group dinners, Mediterranean Nights, cultural trips in Brighton and to London, joint events with other societies at the University of Sussex, coffee mornings, and talks and debates. Joining the Italian Society is a great way to socialise and meet new people, discover the Italian culture and have a lot of fun!

Our History

In year 2017 we raised £400, which we sent to a charity organisation in central Italy to deal with the difficult situation arising from the several earthquakes hitting Italy in 2016.
